About YFAD (Yeast Feature Association Database)

  Yeast feature association database provides comprehensive nexuses between highly descriptive or molecular features to which a list of genes is associated. Here, various gene ontologies, protein domains, mutant phenotypes, pathways, diseases, transcription regulation, physical- and genetic-interactions through browse and search mode could be explored for its associated features. To do so we have utilized the yeast genes/proteins lists which are attributed with a given term (e.g. GO, protein domains or pathways etc.,), and systematic level enrichment analysis based association was established using hypergeometric test. Furthermore, this user-friendly feature-association database has the potential to expand and decipher the novel molecular linkages of the yeast biology including human disease representatives in the yeast model systems.
